JUNGLE TIGERS
NFT COLLECTION

We are creating a metaverse in the form of a jungle of animals, in which different classes live. You will need to look for offspring and extract resources, seize foreign lands and protect, pump and breed them in order to sell or stake. We will have a whole series of nft animals — tigers, monkeys, rhinoceroses, snakes, crocodiles, giraffes, elephants, birds and others — this is an exciting jungle metacapture!
